Multi-genre EDM producer and DJ

Just Nieman is an Asheville-based multi‑genre EDM producer, DJ, and event curator with deep roots in building community on the dancefloor. Since first stepping behind the decks in 2013, he’s gone from throwing DIY house parties to holding down club residencies and running his own event production company, Pluto Events.

Known for his high‑energy sets that blend underground grit with feel‑good club euphoria, Just Nieman crafts experiences designed to connect people. With Pluto Events, he’s created a platform that champions emerging artists, nurtures Asheville’s electronic scene, and brings together crowds for nights they’ll never forget.

In the studio, his releases like “Lives,” “Personal,” “My Music,” and “It’s Not That Deep” showcase an evolving sound that crosses genres while staying true to his passion for movement and emotion. For Just Nieman, it’s never just about playing tracks — it’s about telling a story, building a vibe, and making every moment on the floor count.
